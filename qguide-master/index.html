Where to get Freebies?

Now that you're in the game, you'll find yourself in Northern Flarine Go find [Novelist] Low Tier NPC which is located here this is where you get your first items to help you level up and farm for better gears

NOTE: Freebies costs 1 penya so you might want to go outside Flarine town and kill an Aibatt first

Where and How to level?

Now you know where to get your freebies, next is to go to the Center Flarine and get some buffs from Buffpang just beside Coupang(red penguin) to boost your stats/damage.. Buffs have 5hrs duration..

Now thats done head over outside Flarine and kill some Aibatt and instantly your level 40 aweasome right? Don't forget to pick up penya.. next is to grab those freebies :D

After getting your freebies stuff teleport to Saint City and level up there till level 60 and teleport now to Darkon 2 you can press 'M' to view the map and click the monster icon to view what mobs are found in that area.. You can level here till your 80 then next we head to Darkon 3 and level up till you reach 120 (with 99.99% exp) once you have that you'll revert back to level 60 Master after this just do the same process till you reach 120 again and teleport now to Bahara Desert and level up there till you reach 150.. now we need to teleport to Plumeria and level there until you reach max level (200)

NOTE:(Lignorinos/Cochlea/Fungus) are the only monsters for now in Plumeria that gives exp and drops perins too:)

What effects does the couple buff give?

  • Power of Love: Increased Attack +250% and Increased Hp +250%
  • Blessing of Love: All stat +1000
  • Miracle of Love: Lucky box +1

What effect does the raised pets give?

  • [Tiger] = STR
  • [Lion] = STA
  • [Rabbit] = DEX
  • [Fox] = INT
  • [Dragon] = Increased Attack %
  • [Griffin] = Increased MP%
  • [Unicorn] = Increased HP%

Where to get Tier 1 items?

Tier 1 NPCs are located at center of Flarine , you can get your Tier 1 Armor,Weapon,Fashion,Cloaks,Masks, Shards Shards gives:
+Additional All Stats
+Increased Attack%
+Increased HP%
+Increased MP%,Critical Damage
+Lucky Box
Note: You need two pieces in order to get the effect :) , SOFA(Scroll Of Fixed Awakes)

Tier 1 Card NPC for pierced armor and weapons is located at center of Flarine

Tier 1 Accessories NPC is located at center of Flarine

Where and How to get Tier 2 items?

You can get Tier 2 Armors by exchanging your Tier 1 Armor to Coupang which is located here beside Buffpang,for Tier 2 Fashion/Mask/Cloak/Accessories you can exchange them to Mayor of Flarine note that you need additional stuff like Perins, Red Perins and Forsaken Souls

Where to get Forsaken Souls?

You can get Forsaken Souls anywhere between B1-B5 :) at Frosaken Tower which is located here

How to get Tier 3 items?

You can get Tier 3 Armors by exchanging..
2 [Tier 1 Part] + 1 [ Tier 2 Part ] + 3 Beast Quest Token + 3 QI Quest Token + 5k RP + 5k Perin

As for Tier 3 Weapons you need to exchange..
2[Tier 1 Weapon] + 1 [Tier 2 Weapon] + 5 QI Quest Token + 5 Beast Quest Token + 15k RP + 15k Perin
